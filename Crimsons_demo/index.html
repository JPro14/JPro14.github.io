About Us

The Crimsons is a three-piece band formed in August 2019 in Las Vegas, with Krister on vocals and guitar, Brandon on drums, and Bubba on bass. They aim to bring back the Grunge genre with heavy influences from bands like Nirvana, Black Sabbath, and Alice in Chains. Their sound is a mix of melodic and heavy music, and they hope to appeal to fans of grunge, rock, and alternative music. Despite some member changes in 2020, the band has a solid group and is working hard to make a name for themselves.
